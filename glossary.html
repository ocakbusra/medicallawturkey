Medical & Health Law Glossary

Navigating the legal landscape of medical malpractice in Türkiye can be complex. This glossary provides clear English definitions for key legal and medical terms used in Turkish health law.

Glossary Term

Medical Malpractice

Turkish: Tıbbi Uygulama Hatası / Malpraktis

Professional negligence by a healthcare provider (doctor, nurse, or clinic) where the treatment provided falls below the accepted standard of practice in the medical community, resulting in injury or death to the patient.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Health Law

Turkish: Sağlık Hukuku

The branch of law that regulates the rights and obligations of patients, healthcare professionals, and healthcare institutions. It encompasses both civil and criminal liability in medical contexts.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Informed Consent

Turkish: Aydınlatılmış Onam

The legal requirement that a patient must be fully informed about the risks, benefits, potential complications, and alternatives of a medical procedure before agreeing to it. In Türkiye, standard "blanket" forms are often legally insufficient.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Statute of Limitations

Turkish: Zamanaşımı

The legally prescribed time limit within which a malpractice claim must be initiated. In Turkish law, these periods vary depending on whether the case is against a private clinic or a state hospital.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Pecuniary Damages

Turkish: Maddi Tazminat

Compensation awarded for measurable financial losses, including medical expenses for corrective surgery, travel costs, and loss of past or future income due to the injury.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Non-Pecuniary Damages

Turkish: Manevi Tazminat

Compensation intended to provide emotional relief for pain, suffering, distress, and the decrease in the quality of life caused by the medical error.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Complication

Turkish: Komplikasyon

An adverse outcome that occurs despite the doctor adhering to the standard of care. Under Turkish law, doctors are generally not held liable for complications if they properly informed the patient and managed the complication correctly.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Power of Attorney

Turkish: Vekaletname

A formal legal document that allows an international patient to authorize a Turkish attorney to represent them in courts, collect medical records, and manage legal proceedings in Türkiye without the patient needing to be physically present.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Court of Cassation

Turkish: Yargıtay

The highest court in Türkiye for civil and criminal matters. Its decisions (case law) establish important precedents that lower courts must follow in medical malpractice cases.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Forensic Medicine Institute

Turkish: Adli Tıp Kurumu (ATK)

The official state body in Türkiye that provides expert medical reports to courts to determine whether a medical error occurred and the extent of the patient's disability or damage.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Burden of Proof

Turkish: İspat Yükü

The obligation of a party to prove the facts they claim. In Turkish malpractice law, while the patient generally proves the damage, the physician often bears the burden of proving they fulfilled their duty to inform (consent).

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Health Tourism

Turkish: Sağlık Turizmi

The practice of traveling to Türkiye for medical, dental, or cosmetic procedures. This sector is governed by specific regulations that provide additional protections for international patients.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Duty of Care

Turkish: Özen Yükümlülüğü

The legal obligation of a physician to act with the diligence, skill, and care that a reasonably competent doctor in the same specialty would exercise under similar circumstances.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Turkish Code of Obligations

Turkish: Türk Borçlar Kanunu (TBK)

The primary legislation that governs contracts and torts (wrongful acts) in Türkiye, forming the legal basis for most compensation claims arising from medical malpractice.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Mediation

Turkish: Arabuluculuk

A mandatory first step in many Turkish civil cases where a neutral third party helps the patient and the clinic reach a settlement before a lawsuit is filed in court.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Criminal Liability

Turkish: Cezai Sorumluluk

Liability that arises when a medical error constitutes a crime under the Turkish Penal Code, such as "causing injury by negligence" (taksirle yaralama).

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Standard of Care

Turkish: Tıbbi Standart

The level of professional performance that is generally accepted by the medical community as appropriate for a specific procedure or treatment.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Consumer Law

Turkish: Tüketici Hukuku

Legal rules protecting patients as "consumers" of medical services. In many cosmetic surgery cases, Turkish Consumer Courts have jurisdiction, which often provides procedural advantages for the patient.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Medical Board Report

Turkish: Sağlık Kurulu Raporu / Heyet Raporu

A comprehensive medical evaluation issued by a committee of specialists, often used as critical evidence to determine the permanent disability rate or the extent of health damage.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Causation

Turkish: İlliyet Bağı / Nedensellik Bağı

The direct causal link that must be established between the doctor's negligent act and the specific injury suffered by the patient to award compensation.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Permanent Disability Rate

Turkish: Maluliyet Oranı

The officially determined percentage of permanent loss of bodily or mental functions resulting from a medical error, used to calculate future loss of earnings.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Temporary Incapacity

Turkish: Geçici İş Göremezlik

The period during which a patient is unable to work or perform daily activities while recovering from a medically induced injury before reaching maximum medical improvement.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Iatrogenic Injury

Turkish: İyatrojenik Zarar

Any harm, injury, or complication unintentionally caused to a patient by a physician, medical treatment, or diagnostic procedure.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Nosocomial Infection

Turkish: Hastane Enfeksiyonu

An infection acquired by a patient during a hospital stay that was not present or incubating at the time of admission, often raising questions about facility hygiene standards.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Wrong-Site Surgery

Turkish: Yanlış Taraf Ameliyatı

A severe never event in which a surgical procedure is performed on the wrong patient, wrong body part, or the wrong side of the body.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Surgical Negligence

Turkish: Cerrahi İhmal

A failure by a surgeon to exercise the required standard of care during an operation, encompassing technical errors, leaving foreign objects inside patients, or poor surgical planning.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Post-Operative Care

Turkish: Ameliyat Sonrası Bakım Yükümlülüğü

The legal and ethical obligation of a healthcare provider to monitor, treat, and manage a patient's recovery and any complications immediately following surgery.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Off-Label Drug Use

Turkish: Endikasyon Dışı İlaç Kullanımı

The practice of prescribing a medication for an unapproved indication, age group, dose, or form of administration, which carries specific informed consent requirements.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Medical Device Liability

Turkish: Tıbbi Cihaz Sorumluluğu

The legal responsibility borne by manufacturers, distributors, or medical facilities when a defective medical implant or device causes patient injury.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Cosmetic Surgery Regulation

Turkish: Estetik Cerrahi Mevzuatı

The specific body of laws and ethical guidelines governing elective aesthetic procedures, which heavily emphasizes guaranteed outcomes and stringent informed consent.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Loss of Earnings

Turkish: Kazanç Kaybı

Pecuniary damages awarded to compensate a patient for the income they have lost, and will predictably lose in the future, due to a malpractice-induced disability.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Future Medical Expenses

Turkish: Gelecekteki Tedavi Giderleri

Compensation intended to cover all anticipated costs for necessary ongoing care, revision surgeries, medications, and rehabilitation resulting from a medical error.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Loss of Consortium

Turkish: Aile Bütünlüğünün Bozulması Zararı

Damages claimed by the close family members or spouse of a malpractice victim for the deprivation of companionship, affection, and marital relations due to severe injury or death.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Wrongful Death

Turkish: Haksız Ölüm

A legal claim brought by surviving dependents or close relatives against a healthcare provider whose negligence directly caused the death of the patient.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Bereavement Damages

Turkish: Ölüm Nedeniyle Manevi Tazminat

Non-pecuniary (moral) compensation awarded to the family members of a deceased patient to alleviate their profound grief and emotional suffering.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Damage Mitigation Obligation

Turkish: Zararı Azaltma Yükümlülüğü

The legal duty of an injured patient to take reasonable steps to prevent their health condition and financial losses from worsening following a medical error.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Interest on Damages

Turkish: Tazminata İşleyen Faiz

The statutory or commercial interest applied to a compensation award, calculated from the date the malpractice occurred or the lawsuit was filed, to preserve the value of the money.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Lump Sum vs. Periodic Payment

Turkish: Toplu / Dönemsel Ödeme

The methods by which courts can order compensation to be paid; while lump-sum is standard in Türkiye, periodic payments (annuities) can be requested for long-term care needs.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Malpractice Insurance

Turkish: Mesleki Sorumluluk Sigortası

Mandatory professional liability insurance for physicians in Türkiye that covers the defense costs and compensation claims arising from medical negligence.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Social Security Recourse

Turkish: SGK Rücu Hakkı

The legal right of the Turkish Social Security Institution (SGK) to reclaim the costs of medical treatments it provided to the victim from the negligent doctor or hospital.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Contractual Liability

Turkish: Sözleşmeden Doğan Sorumluluk

The legal responsibility arising when a healthcare provider or private clinic breaches the explicit or implied terms of the treatment contract with the patient.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Tortious Liability

Turkish: Haksız Fiilden Doğan Sorumluluk

Liability that arises from a wrongful act or infringement of a right (other than under contract) leading to civil legal liability, often applicable in emergency interventions.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Vicarious Liability

Turkish: Üstün Sorumluluğu / Adam Çalıştıranın Sorumluluğu

The legal doctrine holding a hospital or clinic owner strictly liable for the negligent actions committed by their employed doctors or nurses during their duties.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Joint and Several Liability

Turkish: Müteselsil Sorumluluk

A legal rule allowing a patient to pursue a claim for the full amount of damages against any one or all of multiple negligent parties (e.g., both the surgeon and the hospital).

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Strict Liability

Turkish: Kusursuz Sorumluluk

Liability that does not depend on actual negligence or intent to harm; in Turkish medical law, this is exceptionally applied to hospitals regarding facility safety and organizational flaws.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Contributory Negligence

Turkish: Müterafik Kusur

A legal defense arguing that the patient's own actions (such as failing to follow post-operative instructions) contributed to their injury, which may reduce the compensation amount.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Force Majeure

Turkish: Mücbir Sebep

An unforeseeable and unavoidable external event (like an earthquake during surgery) that totally prevents a doctor from fulfilling their obligations, exempting them from liability.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Assumption of Risk

Turkish: Riski Kabul

The legal principle that a patient, having been fully and properly informed of the unavoidable natural risks of a procedure, cannot claim malpractice if one of those specific risks occurs.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Breach of Contract

Turkish: Sözleşmenin İhlali

A failure by a medical provider to perform any term of a contract, such as a cosmetic surgeon failing to achieve an explicitly promised aesthetic outcome.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Medical Service Contract

Turkish: Vekalet / Eser Sözleşmesi

The legal classification of the doctor-patient relationship; generally a mandate contract (vekalet) for healing, but a contract of work (eser) for elective cosmetic results.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Patient Rights Regulation

Turkish: Hasta Hakları Yönetmeliği

The comprehensive administrative legislation in Türkiye detailing the fundamental rights of patients, including the right to information, privacy, and respectful care.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Right to Refuse Treatment

Turkish: Tedaviyi Reddetme Hakkı

The fundamental right of a mentally competent patient to decline or halt medical interventions, even if doing so may result in severe health consequences or death.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Right to Second Opinion

Turkish: İkinci Görüş Hakkı

The legal right of a patient to consult with another physician regarding their diagnosis and treatment plan without facing prejudice from their primary healthcare provider.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Privacy of Medical Data

Turkish: Tıbbi Veri Gizliliği

The strict legal protection of patient health records and personal information, protected under both medical ethics and the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK).

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Right to Access Medical Records

Turkish: Tıbbi Kayıtlara Erişim Hakkı

A patient's absolute right to obtain copies of all documents, test results, and notes in their medical file, which is crucial for building a malpractice case.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Patient Advocate

Turkish: Hasta Hakları Birimi

A dedicated unit within Turkish hospitals designed to address patient grievances, facilitate communication, and mediate minor disputes before they escalate to legal action.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Vulnerable Patient

Turkish: Savunmasız Hasta

Patients who are minors, elderly, mentally incapacitated, or language-barrier-restricted (like tourists), requiring heightened standards of care and specialized consent procedures.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Minor Patient Consent

Turkish: Küçük Hastada Onam

The legal requirement that valid consent for treating a patient under the age of 18 must be obtained from their parents or legal guardians, except in immediate life-saving emergencies.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Capacity to Consent

Turkish: Rıza Ehliyeti

The mental and legal ability of a patient to understand the nature, risks, and consequences of a medical procedure and make an informed decision regarding it.

Read detailed guide → Glossary Term

Emergency Exception to Consent

Turkish: Acil Durumda Onam İstisnası

The legal provision allowing doctors to perform life-saving medical interventions on an unconscious or incapacitated patient without obtaining prior consent.
