Welcome to Medical Law Turkey. This Terms of Use outlines how we operate in accordance with Turkish Law and applicable international regulations.

1. Introduction

Please read this document carefully. By using our services and website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Terms of Use. Our practice is strictly subject to the professional rules and regulations of the Union of Turkish Bar Associations (TBB) and the Attorneyship Law No. 1136.

2. Data Protection and Confidentiality

All legal services, consultations, and medical records are managed strictly under the framework of the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK - Law No. 6698). For our international clients, our data practices align with the core principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • We collect only necessary information to evaluate malpractice claims.
  • Health data (Special Category Data) is processed strictly under express consent.
  • We never share data with third parties without explicit authorization.

3. Legal Services and Jurisdiction

Medical Law Turkey is a law boutique representing patients who have suffered complications in Turkey. Our services are subject to the Turkish Code of Obligations (Türk Borçlar Kanunu) and the jurisdiction of Turkish Courts. We handle claims via an official Power of Attorney (Vekaletname) authorized by Turkish Consulates abroad.

4. Contact the Data Controller

For any questions regarding your rights, data erasure requests, or to learn more about this Terms of Use, please contact our legal team directly across our secure communication channels or via info@medicallawturkey.com.
