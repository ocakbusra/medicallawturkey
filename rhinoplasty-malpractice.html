Understanding Rhinoplasty Malpractice in Türkiye

Rhinoplasty is a cosmetic procedure performed to alter the shape, size, or function of the nose. The procedure may involve aesthetic and functional considerations.

Türkiye is widely chosen for rhinoplasty procedures.

Some patients later describe outcomes that differ from expectations.

Situations Sometimes Described by Patients After Rhinoplasty

  • Shape or proportion not meeting expectations
  • Asymmetry
  • Breathing difficulties
  • Visible scarring or irregularities
  • Prolonged swelling
  • Discussions regarding revision surgery

Legal Framework for Aesthetic Procedures

Rhinoplasty is typically evaluated under eser sozlesmesi where a specific result is discussed.

The Importance of Informed Consent

Patients are generally expected to receive information regarding:

  • Expected shape and proportion
  • Functional considerations
  • Healing timeline and limitations

Information for International Patients

Foreign patients generally have similar rights. Documentation plays a central role.

General Observations on Documentation and Review

  • Pre-operative simulations
  • Medical records
  • Consent forms
  • Photographs
  • Correspondence with the clinic
